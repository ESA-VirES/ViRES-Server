    The website is a visual interface to the Earth Explorer Swarm constellation mission and its magnetic products which are officially released by ESA. It allows the searching for, the analysis of, and the download of Swarm data selected by the user. It includes tools for studying various Earth magnetic models in comparison to the Swarm satellite measurements at given solar activity level. It furthermore includes functions for diagram generation and other means of communication.

  5. 5 Personal data privacy and use of cookies

    The collected User Profile Information (see above) is never shared with a third party with the only exception of the Swarm Mission Management at ESA. The email address is only used to contact the User in a very limited fashion to inform about changes of the VirES Service or to inquire about the user's interest in this service. The User agrees to receiving those emails.

    User Profile Information is stored and processed in Austria and Germany.

    EOX is taking reasonable technical and organisational precautions to prevent the loss, misuse, or alteration of User Profile Information. All requests and responses particularly sign up and log in are encrypted using SSL technology.

    Users are responsible for keeping their password and User details confidential. EOX never asks Users for their passwords, except, of course, in the log in form of the VirES Service. Passwords are never stored unencrypted.

    Users can provide model coefficient data in order to let the service calculate custom magnetic field models. These coefficients are never stored and cannot be viewed or utilized by EOX or other users.

    Cookies are pieces of information that a website stores on a visitor's device. We distinguish cookies required for the functioning of the service, cookies for statistical analysis (tracking), and third party cookies.

    The required cookies are those used for user authentication which are basically one named "sessionid" and one named "csrftoken". Users not accepting those cookies cannot use the VirES Service for technical reasons. Note that passwords are never stored in cookies.

    EOX uses a self-hosted Piwik installation for statistical analysis. Collected information is never shared with a third party. Only aggregated statistical reports are shared. The collected information is used by EOX to better understand user behaviour and needs. EOX respects the Do Not Track preference configured in user browsers which can be used to opt out of tracking.

    Third party cookies are set by external web pages such as YouTube for their purposes and are neither controlled nor read by EOX.

    The VirES Service stores the following common log data for every request: remote Internet protocol (IP) address, time, requested URL, status, response size, referer if present, and user-agent. The log data is only used for monitoring the VirES Service and never shared with a third party.

    Log data as well as tracking data are deleted monthly after generating aggregated statistical reports. These reports do not allow to reconstruct individual user activities. They are used for reporting and communication towards ESA internal bodies, ESA Member States, and the general public regarding the distribution and use of Swarm data.

    EOX is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of third party websites the VirES Service links to.
